About Fran

Fran Miller opened Fran's Flowers with a simple goal: to make every day more colorful for her community. Her deep love for botanical design and her warm, welcoming spirit are the heart of the shop.

From teaching neighborhood workshops to delivering surprise bouquets, Fran has spent decades turning small moments into joyful memories.

Today she continues to lead with kindness, quality flowers, and a commitment to giving back to San Francisco.
